Today I went to the last day of band camp. I woke up to my dog barking at absolutely nothing and sadly it was 6:30 AM and I had to wake up at 7:00 AM. I decided to let the dog out of my room and into my sisters and tried to fall back asleep but before I knew it, it was already time to wake up. Everyone was there including brass and percussion and it was a beautiful sight to see. around 8:20 AM we started stretching and sadly we had to run the spirit lap. No one was prepared for the spirt lap because we didn't have to do it last week and we had a whole weekend to not be running. After stretching we did basics block for about 30 minutes or so and then a drill-down. I got eliminated because of going from a slide into a backward march. We set drill for about 3 hours and then went inside and ate some pizza for lunch. After our pizza break, we did sectionals and practiced 4/5 pieces of music for our show. We have 3 memorized so far and we are moving onto the 4th piece of music. After that, we went into the lunchroom with everyone and did breathing exercises and played our music, and then had a 10-minute snack break. After our snack break, we went outside and played all 3 pieces of the music and color guard did their thing, and then we went home. Now, all that's left is resting.
